# Neon Window

A **window** to another world where **neon** is the normal.

This piece of art was created as a experiment on simplicity.

* **Language**: *JavaScript*
* **Libraries**: *P5.js* <sup>[1]</sup>
* **Color Schemes**: *Nord*, *Gruvbox*, *Dracula*
